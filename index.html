Quality Standards

What independent verification looks like.

We don't self-certify. Every batch is sent to a third-party analytical lab for complete characterization before it ever reaches inventory.

01
Synthesis & Raw Testing
Crude peptide is synthesized under clean-room conditions and immediately screened for impurities.
02
HPLC Purity Analysis
Reverse-phase HPLC quantifies purity. We reject anything below 99% — no exceptions.
03
Mass Spectrometry Confirmation
LC-MS confirms molecular weight and sequence integrity, catching substitution or truncation errors.
04
Endotoxin & Certificate
Limulus Amebocyte Lysate (LAL) testing ensures endotoxin levels are safe for research use.
