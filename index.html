Add products to cart, choose your crypto (BTC, ETH, SOL, USDC), send payment to the provided wallet address, then email your transaction ID and shipping address to orders@titanpeptidelab.com

Do you ship to my country? +

We ship to 160+ countries including USA, Canada, UK, EU, Australia, and most of Asia. If your country accepts international mail, we can ship there. Customs regulations are your responsibility.

What payment methods do you accept? +

Crypto only: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and USDC. No credit cards, no PayPal, no bank transfers. Crypto ensures privacy for both of us.

Is the packaging discreet? +

Yes. Plain padded envelope or box with no product names, no company logos, no indication of contents. Return address shows "TL Labs" with a generic address.

What if my order is seized by customs? +

We reship once for free if your order is seized. You must provide official customs seizure documentation. Some high-risk countries may not qualify for reship — contact us before ordering.

Every order includes a Certificate of Analysis (COA) with HPLC and mass spec data. You can also send samples to Janoshik or other independent labs for verification.
