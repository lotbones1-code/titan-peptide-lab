Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything researchers need to know before ordering. Can't find your answer? Email support@titanpeptidelab.com

What purity standard do your peptides meet?
All peptides are independently verified to ≥99% purity via HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) and confirmed by mass spectrometry. Every batch undergoes endotoxin testing (<1 EU/vial). A Certificate of Analysis with batch-specific results is included with every order — not a generic document.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We ship to 160+ countries with customs-compliant, discreet packaging. International orders typically arrive in 5-10 business days. All shipments include tracking. Free shipping on orders over $150 (domestic) — international shipping calculated at checkout based on destination.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDC (ERC-20 and Solana), and Solana (SOL). Bank/wire transfer is available for wholesale or institutional orders. Cryptocurrency payments provide faster processing and enhanced privacy.
How should I store the peptides after delivery?
Lyophilized (freeze-dried) peptides should be stored at -20°C for long-term storage or 2-8°C (refrigerator) for short-term. Reconstituted peptides should be refrigerated and used within 30 days. Nasal sprays should be refrigerated after opening. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.
What is your return/refund policy?
If a product arrives damaged or doesn't match the COA specifications, we'll replace it or issue a full refund. Contact support@titanpeptidelab.com with your order ID and photos. Due to the nature of research chemicals, we cannot accept returns on opened or reconstituted products.
Are these peptides for human consumption?
No. All products are sold strictly for in-vitro research and laboratory use only. They are not intended for human or animal consumption, therapeutic use, or diagnostic purposes. By purchasing, you confirm you are 21+ and will use products solely for legitimate scientific research.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?
Yes. We offer tiered pricing for larger research orders. Contact orders@titanpeptidelab.com with your requirements and we'll provide a custom quote within 24 hours. Institutional purchase orders accepted.
